Spicy Potatoes Recipe

  • Time: 25
  • Serves:4
Ingredients Quantity
Potato 6 to 7 medioumsize
Lemon juice 3 teaspoons
Salt to taste
Vinegar 1 teaspoon
Coriander leaves for garnish
Coriander seeds 2 tablespoons
Cardamom brown 2
Cumin seeds 2 tablespoons
Dry red chilly 7 to 8

Method:

Step 1: Wash and peel of the skin of potatoes. Take the peeled potato into pressure cooker along with a glass of water, close the lid of the cooker and pressure cook it on a high flame for one whistle, then keep the flame low and cook for another 6-7 minutes. Switch off the flame, open cooker and strain the water using a strainer. Keep the potato in fridge for 1 hours.

Step 2: Now cut the potato into cubes or slices.

Step 3: Dry roast coriander seeds, cumin seeds, dry red whole chili and cardamom brown till nice aroma comes from the spices. Switch off the flame. Let the spices cools at the room temperature. In a grinder, add roasted spices and dry grind it coarsely.

Step 4: Take the diced potato in a broad bowl, add 1 tbsp ground spices, salt and vinegar.Squeeze lemon juice. Mix it well. Garnish with coriander leaves. Delicious Spicy Potato is ready to serve.

Mango Banana smoothie Recipe

  • Time:10
  • Serves:1
Ingredients Quantity
Mango 1
Sugar 5 tablespoons
Banana 1
Milk 2 cup
Ice cubes 1/2 cup
Tutty fruity for garnish

Method:

Step 1: Combine all the ingredients except tutty fruity in a jar.

Step 2: Grind it till smooth . Pour into a glass and garnish with tutty fruity. Serve chilled.

Step 3: Enjoy!!!

Moong Dal Cheela Recipe

  • Time:30
  • Serves:4
Ingredients Quantity
Moong Dal 1 cup
Turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon
Redchili Powder 1 teaspoon
Asafoetida 1/4 teaspoon
Salt to taste
Capsicum 1/4 cup chopped
Onion 1/4 cup chopped
Tomato 1/4 cup chopped
Green chilli 2
Coriander leaves 2 tbsp chopped

Method:

Step 1: Clean, wash and soak the moong dal for 3-4 hours. Drain the water and place the soaked dal in a grinder and grind it to make a fine paste.

Step 2: Take out the ground dal in a bowl, Cover it and keep it aside for 5-6 hours or overnight for fermentation.

Step 3: Chop the vegetables finely.Take the fermented dal and add 1/4 cup of water, whisk it well till the dal is creamy and the consistency of the dal should be pouring like dosa. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt and asafoetida. Mix it well.

Step 4:Heat the nonstick pan and grease with 1 teaspoon oil.Pour one ladle of the batter and spread it with the help of ladle in a circular motion.Spread the vegetables and 1 teaspoon oil on the Cheela. Cook covered on a low flame for 3-4 minutes or till the other side is light golden in the color.

Step 5:Flip it with the help of spatula and cook from the vegetable sides for another 3-4 minutes. Delicious Cheela is ready to serve, Serve hot with Coconut chutney. Do the same with the rest of the batter.
